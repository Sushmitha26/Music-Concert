Artists

We’re happy to welcome our five artists to present the live concert.Prepare for the musical extravaganza.

Armaan Malik

Armaan Malik (born 22 July 1995) is an Indian singer, songwriter and an actor. He is known for his singing in Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, Tamil, Bengali, Gujarati and Marathi. He also sung in many indian languages.A finalist on Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Little Champs (2006), Armaan advanced to eighth place with public votes. He is the brother of composer Amaal Mallik. Previously represented by Universal Music India, he is now signed on by T-Series. He has also signed with Arishta records.His first on-screen appearance was in the film Kaccha Limboo in the year 2011. The singer also featured as a coach on an Indian reality show, The Voice India Kids , and became the youngest coach/judge in the history of Indian singing reality show.

Shreya Goshal

Shreya Ghoshal (born 12 March 1984) is an Indian singer, composer and music producer. She has received four National Film Awards, four Kerala State Film Awards, two Tamil Nadu State Film Awards, seven Filmfare Awards including six for Best Female Playback Singer and ten Filmfare Awards South. She has recorded songs for film music and albums in various Indian languages and has established herself as a leading playback singer of Indian cinema.At the age of sixteen, she entered and won the television singing reality show Sa Re Ga Ma. Following that, she made her Bollywood playback singing debut with Bhansali's romantic drama Devdas (2002) for which she received a National Film Award, a Filmfare Award for Best Female Playback Singer and a Filmfare RD Burman Award for New Music Talent.
